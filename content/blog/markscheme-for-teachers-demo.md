## Quick answer

**MarkScheme for teachers** ([markscheme.app/for-teachers](https://markscheme.app/for-teachers)) lets Cambridge and IB educators create **classrooms with invite codes**, see **class-wide topic blindspots** from student marking attempts, and **override AI marks** in a review queue. Students use the same [/mark](/mark) engine; teachers get analytics on top. Email hello@markscheme.app for school questions.

## What this demo shows

Teachers do not need a separate marking product — students upload handwriting as usual; the dashboard aggregates where the class loses marks before mocks. This demo walks through creating a class, sharing a code, and reading blindspot + review tools.

**Watch on TikTok:** [@markscheme](https://www.tiktok.com/@markscheme)

## Step-by-step (from the video)

1. **Sign up** for a free MarkScheme account (teacher role).
2. **Open the teacher dashboard** at [markscheme.app/teacher](/teacher) and **create a classroom**.
3. **Share the invite code** — students join from their accounts in one step.
4. **Students mark past papers** at [/mark](/mark) as normal (self-mark first, then second pass).
5. **Open blindspot radar** — see which syllabus topics the class struggles with across attempts.
6. **Use the review queue** — override AI marks when you disagree; your judgement is saved.
7. **Email** hello@markscheme.app for multi-class or school-wide questions.

## Transcript

> Teachers keep asking if MarkScheme is only for students marking alone — it is not.
>
> Go to markscheme.app/for-teachers. You create a classroom and get an invite code. Students join and mark past papers the same way everyone else does at /mark — upload handwriting, get scheme-aligned feedback.
>
> The difference is what you see. The dashboard shows class blindspots — which topics the whole group keeps losing marks on after mocks. There is a grade risk view so you can see who is working hard but still dropping method marks.
>
> And if you disagree with an AI mark on a borderline script, you override it in the review queue. Your call is recorded.
>
> This is for **formative** past-paper practice — students self-mark first, MarkScheme is the second pass. It is not a replacement for your formal assessments or examiner training.
>
> Schools can email hello@markscheme.app with subject list and student numbers. Link in bio if you want to try a class this week.

## Honest limits

MarkScheme is **not endorsed** by Cambridge International or the IBO. It is independent software using published mark schemes and markband language. Not suitable as sole summative grading. Very large schools should contact us for rollout planning.
