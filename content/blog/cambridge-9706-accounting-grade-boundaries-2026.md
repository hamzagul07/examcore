## What are grade boundaries?

Minimum **raw marks** per grade per component. Accounting rewards **correct treatments and shown workings** — method marks often apply when the final figure is wrong.

## How Cambridge sets 9706 boundaries

Set after marking. Thresholds move with paper difficulty so an A means the same standard each year.

## How to estimate your grade
