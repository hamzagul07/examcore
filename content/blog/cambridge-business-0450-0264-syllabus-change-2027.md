School WhatsApp groups mix up **0450** and **0264** every term. Here is the clear version: **if you sit Business in 2026, you are almost certainly on 0450 Business Studies**. The new **0264 Business** syllabus starts assessment from **2027**. Wrong code = wrong past papers = wasted revision hours.

## Quick answer

| Your exam year | Syllabus code | Past papers to use |
|--------------|---------------|-------------------|
| **2026** (March / June / November) | **0450** Business Studies | 0450 past papers to 2025 |
| **2027 onwards** | **0264** Business (new) | 0264 specimen + 0450 for skills overlap only |
| 9–1 route from 2027 | **0774** (replaces 0986) | Check statement of entry |

Source: [Cambridge syllabus changes factsheet](https://www.cambridgeinternational.org/Images/663781-syllabus-changes-international-.pdf).

## Why Cambridge changed the code

Cambridge is refreshing several IGCSE syllabuses for teaching from **2025–2026**, first examined **2027**. Business moves from **Business Studies (0450)** to **Business (0264)** with:

- Updated **syllabus aims** and learner attributes  
- Reordered and trimmed content  
- **New topics** — e.g. technology in production, sustainable production  
- **Digital communication** woven into People in Business  
- Published **formulas and ratios** list in subject content  

The **name and code change** signal a substantive refresh — not a rename only.

## What 2026 students should do

If your exams are in **2026**:

1. Confirm **0450** on your statement of entry  
2. Use **0450** past papers and mark schemes only  
3. Ignore 0264 specimen papers for exam prediction — format may differ  
4. Practise **case study** and **data response** under time — see [data response guide](/blog/cambridge-data-response-questions-guide)  

The 0450 syllabus received a minor version update (December 2025) with **no significant content change** for 2026 exams.

## What 2027 students should do

If you are in the first **0264** cohort (exams from 2027):

| Now (Year 10) | Closer to exams |
|---------------|-----------------|
| Learn content from **0264** teacher materials | Shift to **0264** specimen & future papers |
| Build skills with **0450** short questions (concepts overlap) | Stop relying on 0450 case studies for exact format |
| Mark every answer against a scheme | Use [MarkScheme](/mark) for essay-style business responses |

Your school’s exams officer registers the **0264** code for 2027 entries — not 0450.

## Content overlap (skills that transfer)

Even when topics move, these **exam skills** repeat:

| Skill | How to practise |
|-------|-----------------|
| **Analyse** case data | Past-paper extracts timed 10 min |
| **Evaluate** decisions | Two-sided paragraphs with judgement |
| **Calculate** ratios | Show formula + units |
| **Define** terms precisely | One-mark definitions from mark schemes |
| **Apply** to context | Never generic textbook paragraphs |

Business marks are lost to **generic answers**, not missing one 2027 topic early.

## 9–1 Business (0986 → 0774)

UK-style **9–1** IGCSE Business follows parallel code changes:

- **0986** last exams **2026**  
- **0774** first exams **2027**  

Same rule: match past papers to **your entry code**, not your friend’s school.

## FAQ

### My textbook says 0450 but teacher mentioned 0264

You are probably being **introduced** to the new syllabus early while still examined on 0450 in 2026. Ask: “Which code is on my **June 2026** entry?”

### Can I use MarkScheme for Business?

Yes — upload case-study responses and data questions; marking follows official **band descriptors** in the scheme.

### Is Business going digital in 2026?

Not in the first digital wave. MCQ digital pilots focus on Accounting/Economics/science MCQ papers — see [digital exams guide](/blog/cambridge-digital-exams-june-2026-student-guide).

### What about O-Level Business 7115?

Separate syllabus — see [7115 guide](/blog/cambridge-7115-o-level-business-studies-past-papers-guide).

## Bottom line

**2026 = 0450. 2027 = 0264.** Check your statement of entry before downloading papers. Skills beat speculation — analyse cases, mark strictly, and fix generic writing. That workflow works on any code and on [MarkScheme](/mark).
