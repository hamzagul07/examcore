## What are grade boundaries?

Minimum **raw marks** per grade — not fixed percentages. Your **overall** grade uses the weighted sum of entered components.

## How Cambridge sets 0460 boundaries

Comparable outcomes after marking — harder papers usually mean lower thresholds.
