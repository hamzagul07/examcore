## What are grade boundaries?

Grade boundaries link your **raw score** on each paper to the grade awarded. For **9709**, each component (e.g. Pure 1 `9709/11`, Mechanics `9709/42`) has its own thresholds; weighted totals give your overall AS or A Level grade.

## How Cambridge sets 9709 boundaries

Boundaries are set **after** marking — not before the exam. If a paper is harder than last year, thresholds typically **drop** so a grade A means the same standard. This is why you cannot aim for a fixed percentage.
