## What are grade boundaries?

Minimum **raw marks** on each component and on your **aggregate** (combined papers). Cambridge uses raw marks, not UMS — every M mark you show counts.

## How Cambridge sets 9231 boundaries

Set after marking. Separate overall thresholds exist for **Pure+Mechanics** vs **Pure+Statistics** routes. Harder papers usually mean lower thresholds.

## How to estimate your grade

### Are Further Maths boundaries higher than 9709?

Not necessarily — papers are harder; raw **percentages** for top grades can be lower than single maths.

**No** — Cambridge sets separate aggregate thresholds per valid paper combination.
