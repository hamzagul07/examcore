**9231 Further Mathematics** extends **9709** culture: proof verbs matter, chains are longer, and **Hence** links are strict.

## Verbs unique to Further Maths

| Verb | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **Prove** | Formal logic — every step justified |
| **Show that** | Arrive at the printed answer with no gaps |
| **Hence** | Must use the prior result — no parallel methods |
| **Deduce** | Logical consequence from given information |
