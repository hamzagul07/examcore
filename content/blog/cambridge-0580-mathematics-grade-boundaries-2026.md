## What are grade boundaries?

Minimum **raw marks** per grade — not fixed percentages. Your **overall** grade uses the weighted sum of your entered components (e.g. Extended route **22 + 42**).

## How Cambridge sets 0580 boundaries

Comparable outcomes after marking — harder papers usually mean lower thresholds. **A*** is awarded on the **aggregate**, not per component.

## Core vs Extended at a glance

| Tier | Papers | Grade range |
|------|--------|-------------|
| Core | Paper 1 + Paper 3 | C to G |
| Extended | Paper 2 + Paper 4 | A* to E |

Confirm your entry with your exams officer — mixing tiers is not allowed.
