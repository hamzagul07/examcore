## What are grade boundaries?

The minimum **raw mark** for each grade on a paper or the full qualification. Biology marking rewards **exact terminology** — compare your script to the official scheme, not your memory of the topic.

## How Cambridge sets 9700 boundaries

Set **after** all marking. Harder papers → lower thresholds, so a grade A means the same ability standard year to year. Past sessions are guides, not predictions.
