## What are grade boundaries?

Minimum **raw marks** per grade per component. Business rewards **application to the case** — naming the business and using stimulus data beats generic theory.

## How Cambridge sets 9609 boundaries

Set after marking. Boundaries move with paper difficulty so an A means the same standard each year. Essay bands: [marking business answers](/blog/marking-a-level-economics-essays-at-home) (similar LoR skills).
