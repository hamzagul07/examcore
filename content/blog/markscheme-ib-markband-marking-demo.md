## Quick answer

**MarkScheme** ([markscheme.app](https://markscheme.app)) marks **handwritten IB Diploma** answers with **markband-style criterion feedback** — not a single vague grade. Self-assess against the official markband column first, then upload photos at [Mark a paper](/mark) as a second pass. Deep guide: [AI marking IB past papers](/blog/ai-marking-ib-past-papers-guide).

## What this demo shows

IB marking is not a checklist of content points — examiners place your answer in a **band** using descriptor language (e.g. "clear analysis" vs "perceptive analysis"). This demo shows photographing one IB response and getting feedback anchored to those criteria, across HL, SL, and Core subjects where marking is enabled.

**Watch on TikTok:** [@markscheme](https://www.tiktok.com/@markscheme) (IB marking demos on the profile).

## Step-by-step (from the video)

1. **Attempt under timed conditions** — treat it like the real paper (command term, word limit, structure).
2. **Self-place into a markband** — read the official column and highlight which descriptor phrases your answer actually meets.
3. **Photograph your script** — one clear image per question or page.
4. **Open** [markscheme.app/mark](/mark) — choose IB Diploma and your subject.
5. **Upload and wait** — criterion-style comments in ~30 seconds per question.
6. **Compare** — did you award yourself a band you did not earn? Redo one skill (application, synthesis, evaluation).

## Honest limits

MarkScheme is **not endorsed by the IBO**. Markbands are holistic; no tool replaces an experienced teacher on borderline scripts. Very illegible handwriting may not be readable. Subject-specific papers differ — a Psychology ERQ is not an Economics Paper 1 case response. Use [IB markbands explained](/blog/ib-markbands-explained) and your subject paper guide before you trust any second opinion.
