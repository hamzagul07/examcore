Business revision clicks when you treat papers as **repeatable mark patterns** — case application, break-even, cash flow, and evaluation paragraphs. This guide ranks high-frequency topics across **9609** (A-Level) and **7115** (O-Level) from **2019—2025**.

> **Key takeaway:** Business marks come from **stimulus data in your answer** — generic theory alone caps your band.

## How this list was built

We weighted frequency on structured and case papers, examiner report comments, and LoR band traps.

## A-Level Business (9609)

| Priority | Topic | Why it repeats |
|----------|-------|----------------|
| 1 | **Marketing mix (4Ps/7Ps)** | Case application every series |
| 2 | **Break-even & contribution** | Calculation + interpret on case data |
| 3 | **Cash flow forecasting** | Table completion and improvement advice |
| 4 | **Leadership & motivation theories** | Named theory + applied to case workforce |
| 5 | **External environment (PEST)** | Scan case context before recommendations |
| 6 | **Operations & quality** | Capacity, JIT, TQM linked to scenario |
| 7 | **Finance ratios** | Interpret accounts in the case |
| 8 | **Strategic choice / Ansoff** | Evaluation of growth options |
