## Quick answer

**IB marking** needs **markband descriptor language**, not generic essay grades. **MarkScheme** ([markscheme.app/mark](/mark)) gives criterion-style feedback on uploaded handwriting for IB Diploma HL, SL, and Core, alongside free [IB courses](/ib/courses). Use it as a **second pass** after you self-assess against the official markband column — not as a replacement for reading the subject guide.

Generic ChatGPT often praises structure while missing whether your answer fits the **best-fit band**. That is the same failure mode as generous self-marking.

## What good IB AI marking looks like

Useful feedback for IB revision:

- References **assessment objectives** (AO1–AO3) and **command terms** for that paper
- Quotes **markband descriptors** your answer hits or misses
- Anchors comments to **your uploaded script**, not a model essay
- Separates **Paper 1 / 2 / 3** demands (e.g. case study application vs ERQ depth)

Weak feedback:

- A single numeric grade with no band evidence
- Generic “good analysis” without criterion mapping
- Ignoring **HL extensions** or word limits

## Where students get misled

1. **Markbands are holistic** — models default to mid-band safe scores.  
2. **Subject-specific criteria differ** — an Economics Paper 1 case response is not a Psychology ERQ.  
3. **Training data ? your session’s guide** — syllabus changes (e.g. 2025 sciences) matter.

## A healthy workflow

1. Attempt under timed conditions.  
2. Self-place into a markband using the official column — highlight descriptor phrases.  
3. Run **MarkScheme** on the same upload for a second read.  
4. Redo **one** skill (application, synthesis, evaluation) — not the whole portfolio.

Pair with [IB markbands explained](/blog/ib-markbands-explained) and subject paper guides (e.g. [Economics Paper 1](/blog/ib-economics-paper-1-guide), [Psychology ERQ](/blog/ib-psychology-erq-essay-guide)).

| **Revision Village** | IB Maths question volume | Worked solutions, not your script |

Revision Village is excellent for **maths question reps**; MarkScheme targets **your written responses** across Diploma subjects plus [free IB courses](/ib/courses).

## Ethics

- **Published past papers & practice** — fair game.  
- **Live exams or IA drafts under school rules** — follow your coordinator’s AI policy.  
- **Academic integrity** — never upload live assessment materials.

## Frequently asked questions

### Can AI mark IB TOK or the Extended Essay?
Use AI to brainstorm or clarify criteria, but long-form Core work needs human supervision. MarkScheme’s [TOK](/ib/courses/tok) and [EE](/ib/courses/extended-essay) courses teach structure; criterion marking on shorter practice tasks helps before drafts.

### Is MarkScheme only for IB Sciences?
No — Business Management, Psychology, Economics, languages, maths, and more have courses and marking support. Browse [/ib/courses](/ib/courses).

### Where is the Cambridge equivalent?
See [AI marking for Cambridge past papers](/blog/ai-marking-cambridge-past-papers-guide).

## Bottom line

IB rewards **best-fit band judgments**. AI helps when it forces you to quote descriptors — not when it hands you a comforting number. Self-mark strictly, second-pass on [MarkScheme](/mark), then fix one gap before the next paper.
