## What are grade boundaries?

Minimum **raw marks** per grade per component. Sociology rewards **named theorists applied to the question** — lists without explanation cap your band.

## How Cambridge sets 9699 boundaries

Set after full marking. Thresholds move with paper difficulty so an A means the same standard each year. Essay bands: [command words](/blog/cambridge-9699-sociology-command-words-guide).

## How to estimate your grade

1. Timed paper per component type  
2. Mark to **band descriptors** — especially evaluation paragraphs
