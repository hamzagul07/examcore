## What are grade boundaries?

Minimum raw marks for each grade. Economics essays use **level-of-response** bands — your total still converts to a raw mark compared against session thresholds.

## How 9708 papers are marked

| Paper | Style |

## Frequently asked questions

### Is A* harder now?

Awarding has normalised post-2022 — standards are criterion-based, not a fixed top-% quota.

### Can one weak paper ruin my grade?

A-Level is **compensatory** — strong P4 essays can offset a weaker P3 MCQ if overall raw total is high enough.
