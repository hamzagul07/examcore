## Quick answer

**Exam Room** is MarkScheme's free student community at [markscheme.app/community](https://markscheme.app/community) — Reddit-style rooms for **Cambridge A-Level and IB Diploma** subjects. Ask past-paper doubts, share notes and PDFs, and discuss grade boundaries. Read without an account; post with a free MarkScheme username.

## What this demo shows

Revision is not only marking and courses — students also need a place to ask "was my working enough for M1?" or "what did boundaries look like last year?" Exam Room gives each subject its own board (e.g. `s/9702` for Physics, `s/math-aa-hl` for IB Maths AA HL).

**Watch on TikTok:** [@markscheme](https://www.tiktok.com/@markscheme)

## Step-by-step (from the video)

1. **Open** [markscheme.app/community](/community) — pick Cambridge or IB on the feed.
2. **Find your subject room** — browse [all subjects](/community/subjects) or search from the hub.
3. **Read hot / new / top** — see doubts, resources, and boundary threads without signing in.
4. **Create a free account** — pick a username to post, comment, or upvote.
5. **Post a doubt or resource** — Discussion for questions; Resource for PDFs/images (see [guidelines](/community/guidelines)).
6. **Pair with marking** — fix the skill at [/mark](/mark), then bring specific questions back to Exam Room.

## Honest limits

Exam Room is **student peer support**, not official advice from Cambridge or the IBO. Answers are not examiner-verified. Do not share leaked exam content. For scheme-aligned feedback on your script, use [/mark](/mark) rather than hoping a comment guesses your marks.
