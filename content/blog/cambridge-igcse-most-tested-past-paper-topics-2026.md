IGCSE students search “what comes up most” every May. The honest answer: **algebra, electrolysis, electricity, and mole calculations** dominate year after year — but only **marked past papers** turn that into grades. Here are the highest-frequency topics for four core IGCSE syllabuses sitting in **2026**, plus a practice sequence that feeds straight into [MarkScheme](/mark).

## Mathematics 0580 (Extended)

Based on 0580 papers **2019–2025** and examiner reports:

| Rank | Topic cluster | Exam habit to fix |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| 1 | **Algebra** (expand, factorise, solve) | Sign errors when factorising |
| 2 | **Number** (%, ratio, standard form) | Calculator mode / rounding |
| 3 | **Graphs** (linear, quadratic, interpret) | Gradient vs intercept confusion |
| 4 | **Geometry** (angles, circles, similarity) | Missing reasons in proofs |
| 5 | **Trigonometry** | Wrong side labels; premature rounding |
| 6 | **Statistics & probability** | Misreading class intervals |
| 7 | **Mensuration** | Unit conversion |
| 8 | **Vectors & transformations** | Direction errors |
| 9 | **Sequences** | nth term vs position |
| 10 | **Indices & surds** | Illegal cancellation |

**Paper split:** Core skills hit Papers 2 and 4; Extended adds depth on 4. Track **Paper 4** separately — it carries the most A/A* leverage.

Practise: [IGCSE past papers guide](/blog/cambridge-igcse-past-papers-guide) + timed Paper 2/4 pairs.

## Biology 0610

| Rank | Topic | Why examiners return to it |
|------|-------|---------------------------|
| 1 | **Enzymes** | Graphs, denaturation, optimum conditions |
| 2 | **Plant nutrition & transport** | Transpiration, xylem/phloem |
| 3 | **Human systems** (digestion, gas exchange) | Multi-mark structured answers |
| 4 | **Disease & immunity** | Vaccination, antibodies |
| 5 | **Coordination & homeostasis** | Hormones, nervous system |
| 6 | **Inheritance** | Genetic diagrams, variation |
| 7 | **Ecology** | Food webs, population graphs |
| 8 | **Biotechnology** | Limited syllabus but high mark density when it appears |
| 9 | **Reproduction** | Comparative plant/animal |
| 10 | **Practical skills (Paper 6)** | Variables, graphs, conclusions |

**Leverage point:** Paper 4 structured questions often decide A vs A*. Give it **40%** of biology past-paper time even though Papers 2/6 feel easier.
