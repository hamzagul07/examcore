Scoring highly in IB Biology HL is not about memorising more — it is about aligning your answers with what examiners reward in the **markbands**. Strategic use of [IB Biology past papers](/ib/past-papers/biology-hl) under timed conditions, honest self-marking, and targeted feedback closes the gap between a 5 and a 7.

## Understanding the IB Biology HL assessment
**Paper 1** is multiple choice across core and AHL. **Paper 2** mixes data-based questions with structured and extended responses. **Paper 3** covers practical skills and your option topic. The **IA** is an individual investigation. Browse the full subject overview at [IB Biology HL](/ib/subjects/biology-hl).

## Markbands and what examiners reward
Extended Paper 2 responses use **markbands** — top bands need synthesis, precise terminology, and evaluation, not fact lists.

## A past paper workflow that actually works
Cycle timed papers → mark MCQs and band long answers → drill weak AHL topics before the next paper. Track command-term losses separately from content gaps.

## Paper-specific tips
For DBQs, describe trends with quoted data first, then explain biology. Paper 3 option: learn standard diagrams for your option.

## Common pitfalls
Ignoring AHL depth; rote recall without application; weak option revision; IA left until the final term.

## Using MarkScheme for targeted feedback
Self-marking against band descriptors is essential, but extended responses benefit from a second opinion. After a past paper or IA section, [get criterion-based feedback](/mark?subject=ib-biology-hl) aligned with IB assessment objectives — the same habits that lift exam scripts also sharpen coursework drafts.

## Frequently asked questions

### HL vs SL papers?
HL adds AHL content and greater depth on Paper 2/3 — use HL papers for timing, not SL-only mocks.

### How many past papers?
6–8 fully marked HL papers with error logs beats rushing through twenty.

### Free course?
Use our [Biology HL course](/ib/courses/biology-hl) for topic refresh between papers.

## Bottom line
Treat [IB Biology HL past papers](/ib/past-papers/biology-hl) as training for examiner thinking — not a trivia test. Cycle timed practice, honest band marking, and focused drills on your weakest criteria; that is how top candidates make a 7 predictable rather than lucky.
