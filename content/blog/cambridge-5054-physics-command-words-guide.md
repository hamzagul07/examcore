**5054 Physics** mixes **Calculate** (quantitative) with **Describe** and **Explain** (qualitative). The command word is the difference between a 1-mark recall and a 4-mark developed answer.

## Core verbs

| Verb | 5054 habit |
|------|------------|
| **Calculate** | Formula, substitution, unit, s.f. |
| **Describe** | Trend, shape, direction — no mechanism yet |
| **Explain** | Physics law or principle causing the description |
| **Suggest** | Reasonable prediction for a new setup |
| **Define** | Textbook definition — units if relevant |

## Graph questions

**Describe** the graph first; **Explain** only when the verb asks why the gradient or intercept means something physically.

## FAQ

### Show working on every Calculate?
Yes — method marks are common; final-only answers risk zero.

### Suggest vs Explain?
**Suggest** allows plausible ideas; **Explain** needs established physics reasoning.
