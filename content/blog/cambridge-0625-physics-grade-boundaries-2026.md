## What are grade boundaries?

Minimum **raw marks** per component and overall  not fixed percentages. **A*** is awarded on the **aggregate**, not per paper.

## How Cambridge sets 0625 boundaries

Comparable outcomes after marking  harder papers usually mean lower thresholds.
